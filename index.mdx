<Note>
🌙 **Casa de Historias** (House of Stories) is a bilingual Android application dedicated to preserving the rich oral storytelling tradition of indigenous communities in the Sierra de Zongolica, Veracruz, Mexico.
</Note>

## About the Project

Casa de Historias bridges the gap between ancestral wisdom and modern technology, providing a platform where community members can discover, share, and preserve legends in both Spanish and Nahuatl. Each story includes audio narration, geolocation data, and cultural context to keep these traditions alive for future generations.

## The Mission

This project serves as a digital archive for indigenous oral traditions, ensuring that the legends, myths, and cultural knowledge of the Sierra de Zongolica communities are preserved and accessible to future generations while honoring the original Nahuatl language alongside Spanish translations.
