# Casa de Historias

Casa de Historias is a bilingual Android application dedicated to preserving and sharing indigenous legends from the Sierra de Zongolica region of Mexico. The app provides a platform for communities to document their oral traditions in both Spanish and Nahuatl, ensuring these valuable cultural narratives are preserved for future generations.

## Mission

Oral traditions form the backbone of indigenous cultural identity, yet they are at risk of being lost as languages decline and communities modernize. Casa de Historias addresses this challenge by:

- **Preserving oral histories** through digital documentation
- **Supporting bilingual access** with content in Spanish and Nahuatl
- **Connecting communities** by mapping stories to their geographic origins
- **Enabling audio preservation** with voice recordings from original narrators
- **Democratizing storytelling** by allowing community members to contribute

## Core Concepts

### Stories

Stories are the heart of Casa de Historias. Each story represents a legend, myth, or traditional tale from the Sierra de Zongolica region.

<Info>
  Every story includes bilingual content with fields for both Spanish (`titleEs`, `contentEs`) and Nahuatl (`titleNahuatl`, `contentNahuatl`) versions.
</Info>

A story in the app contains:

```kotlin
data class Story(
    val id: String,
    val titleEs: String,
    val titleNahuatl: String,
    val contentEs: String,
    val contentNahuatl: String,
    val audioUrl: String?,
    val imageUrl: String?,
    val narratorName: String,
    val community: String,
    val latitude: Double? = null,
    val longitude: Double? = null,
    val authorId: String = "",
    val tags: List<String> = emptyList(),
    val createdAt: Long = 0L
)
```

### Communities

Each story is associated with a specific community in the Sierra de Zongolica. Communities represent the geographic and cultural origins of the stories, allowing users to:

- Browse stories by their community of origin
- See where stories originated on an interactive map
- Understand the cultural context of different regions

<Card title="Discover by Community" icon="map-location-dot" href="#">
  Browse stories organized by their communities of origin, from Tequila to Atlanca and beyond.
</Card>

### Narrators

Narrators are the storytellers and culture bearers who share these legends. Each story preserves the name of the person who contributed the narrative, honoring their role in cultural preservation.

<Tip>
  Many stories include audio recordings (`audioUrl`) featuring the original narrator's voice, preserving not just the words but also the authentic oral delivery.
</Tip>

## Technology Stack

Casa de Historias is built using modern Android development tools:

- **Kotlin** - Primary programming language
- **Jetpack Compose** - Modern declarative UI framework
- **Firebase** - Backend services including Firestore, Authentication, and Storage
- **Room Database** - Local data persistence for offline access
- **Hilt** - Dependency injection
- **MapLibre** - Interactive mapping
- **Media3** - Audio playback

<Note>
  The app supports Android 8.0 (API level 26) and above, ensuring broad compatibility across devices.
</Note>
